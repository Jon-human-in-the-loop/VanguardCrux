The Challenge

E-commerce and social media teams need dozens of ad variations weekly: different angles, messaging, audiences, platforms. Traditional production requires casting, filming, editing—weeks of work and thousands per video. Scaling demand outpaces production capacity. Budget constraints force brands to reuse stale content. Performance metrics languish because iteration is impossible.

Ad performance depends on constant variation testing. A/B tests demand 10+ creative variants. Winning hypotheses need rapid iteration. The bottleneck: humans. Casting actors, negotiating availability, managing shoots, supervising edits. Each video is a project, not a commodity.

The solution required a factory: automated content production where inputs are trends + brand guidelines, outputs are finished videos, and human labor approaches zero.

The Solution

Zero-Touch Content Orchestration

n8n Workflow Engine

Orchestrates the entire production pipeline. Ingests trends, brand context, and audience segments. Triggers AI stages in sequence. Monitors quality gates. Delivers final videos directly to cloud storage without human intervention.

Pinecone RAG Context

Vectorial memory of brand voice, campaign history, and audience insights. RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) feeds relevant context into script generation. Ensures consistency across variants while maintaining freshness and personalization.

Gemini 2.5 Flash Scripts

Generates persuasive scripts tailored to platform, audience, and trend. Produces variations in seconds. Context-aware copy avoids repetition. Output is production-ready: pacing, tone, call-to-action all optimized.

HeyGen Digital Avatars

Photorealistic synthetic presenters eliminate casting and talent logistics. Avatars speak in multiple languages. Lip-sync is perfect. Visual consistency across campaigns guaranteed. Performance equals professional actors at zero scheduling overhead.

Enterprise Scalability

Input: 1 brief. Output: 20 video variants in hours. Variations span platforms (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube), audiences (segments A/B/C), and angles (benefit-driven, social-proof, urgency). Each video is unique. Each is broadcast-quality. Each ships instantly to CDN.

Cost per video drops from $1000+ to $30-50. Timeline collapses from weeks to hours. Campaigns that required 30 person-days now require 1 briefing session. The factory runs 24/7. Production is infinite. Bottleneck moves from execution to creative strategy.
