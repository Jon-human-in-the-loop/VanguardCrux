Maia Kode

AI-Driven Digital Persona Validation

Proof-of-concept framework validating influencer AI viability before capital investment. Used Gemini LLM to analyze behavioral patterns of 16 industry leaders, synthesizing optimal persona traits. Reduced market validation risk and accelerated full-scale Maia Kode development.

Gemini API LLM Analysis Voice Synthesis

The Challenge

EdTech brand considering $500K+ investment in virtual influencer AI. Uncertain which personality traits, communication style, and visual aesthetic would resonate in STEM education niche. Design assumptions untested. Risk of building wrong persona and wasting capital.

Building a virtual influencer requires decisions across multiple dimensions: personality archetype, tone of voice, visual identity, content approach. Each dimension affects audience retention differently. Wrong choices cost months and capital. No data exists to guide the design.

The solution required a lightweight PoC: extract patterns from proven industry figures, synthesize an optimal persona, validate via prototyping. Reduce risk before committing full development budget.

The Solution

Data-Driven Persona Engineering

Influencer Analysis Database

Curated 16 leading STEM educators and EdTech influencers. Structured data across 50+ traits: communication style, humor frequency, technical depth, accessibility, engagement patterns. Cross-referenced audience retention metrics and follower growth patterns.

Gemini LLM Pattern Inference

Processed qualitative data through Google Gemini API. LLM extracted behavioral archetypes, personality dimensions, and communication frameworks. Identified patterns correlating with high audience retention. Generated synthesis report mapping traits to audience preferences.

Voice Synthesis Iteration

Built Flask microservice integrating TTS engines. Rapid prototyping of 8+ voice variants generated by LLM prompts. A/B tested voice characteristics against audience retention predictions. Selected optimal tone and speech patterns.

Persona Specification Artifact

Synthesized findings into executable persona specification: personality archetype, voice parameters, content approach, visual identity direction, engagement framework. Handed to full-scale development team as validated blueprint reducing assumption-based design risk.

Validation Through Rapid Prototyping

Created interactive dashboard presenting persona recommendations with supporting evidence. Visualized 16-influencer trait distributions. Displayed voice synthesis samples. Showed retention correlation analysis. Stakeholders reviewed in one session, approved persona specification, greenlighting full-scale development.

Time to market: 3 weeks from project start to validated persona. Capital efficiency: $40K PoC investment saved $250K+ in misdirected development. Risk reduction: design decisions now backed by data analysis rather than assumption.
